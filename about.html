About The Gratitude Journal

The Gratitude Journal is a digital journalling app that allows you to write your daily gratitudes without a need for a physical journal. It is an easy way to write down each day what you are grateful for at the same time helping to improve your mood.

You can write up to 7 gratitudes each week. One for every day to reflect on your week and feel good about life.

Your wellbeing is important. Use gratitude journalling to feel better and learn to think more positively.
